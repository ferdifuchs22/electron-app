Hello and welcome to DraftRec! This tool will help you choose the right champion your team needs to win the game, while allowing you to complete the entire champion select process using the tool itself! The recommendations are created using the latest statistics from lolalytics.com and consider champion winrates, counter winrates, teammate synnergies and which type of champion your team needs. To get started, simply keep DraftRec open and jump into any live Draft!
